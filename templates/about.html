What is FlipHelper?

"Flipping" items is blah blah blah... Buy low sell high... Introductory stuff.

The problem is that it takes time and experience to start flipping items effectively. It can also be difficult to get started and take full advantage of garage sale offerings without knowledge of a wide range of product categories. FlipHelper is your yard sale advisor, bringing you the latest product values from the top online resale marketplaces.


How it Works

Enter the name or description of an item you find at a yard sale/thrift store/laying around the house (the more descriptive, the better!). Also enter the asking price, or what you think it's worth if you already own it. If applicable, select a category that the item falls into. This serves to exclude irrelevant results that may match your search terms and to include some niche, category-specific marketplaces.

Hit search, and FlipHelper finds the most recent online listings that match your search terms, across a number of sites such as eBay, Etsy, and Reverb. Based on the most recent sold and active listings, FlipHelper lets you know if what you're looking at has potential to be flipped for profit.

FlipHelper's insights into market pricing take the guesswork out of yard sale flipping, and provide you with immediate intuition and insider knowledge about any item you find.
